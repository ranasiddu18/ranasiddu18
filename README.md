I'm a final-year Electronics and Communication Engineering undergraduate with hands-on experience across the digital design flow — from **RTL coding in Verilog HDL** to **functional verification, simulation, synthesis, and hardware validation** on Xilinx Vivado and Artix-7 FPGA hardware. My work centers on writing synthesizable RTL, building custom testbenches, debugging waveforms, and validating designs on real silicon-adjacent hardware.

I'm currently sharpening my verification and RTL-to-GDSII fundamentals while actively solving digital design problems on HDLBits, and I'm looking to bring that foundation into an industry RTL Design, VLSI, or Design Verification role.
